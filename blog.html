Inauguration of WOW

Inauguration of winning over women's (WOW) cancer express in Gulmohar park,New Delhi on november 24, 2012 by Dr. Kiran Walia the, then honourable eduction minister and former minister of Health & Family welfare,department of WCD, Govt. of nct of Delhi.

Discussion on Health Equity

Discussion meeting on Health Equity for people in Gautam Budh Nagar, u.p. on December 29, 2012, at its office in Greater Noida with medical officers of PHCS located in GBN; village pradhans, GBN; principals, govt. schools in GBN besides invitees from WTI, DST; the ministry of drinking water & sanitation, and the GBN district TB cell.

Community screening for detecting abnormalities.

Community screening for detecting suspect women having abnormalities in the cervix and breast, indicative of cervical cancer and breast cancer (venue: b-block, community center, new Rajender Nagar) on February 9, 2013, with WOW cancer express.

Four prostate disease awareness programs

Community sensitization on women’s cancers

Community sensitization on women’s cancers and use of primary screening methods to detect suspect women having abnormalities in cervix and breast on April 17, 2013, at the community health center (CHC), Bhangel, sector-110, Noida.

Eight vermicomposting pits

Eight demonstrational vermicomposting pits (size: 8 feet × 2 feet) was set up where earthworms (Eisenia fetida) were cultured for converting the bio-waste into nutrient-rich remains in the form of worm castings or vermicompost in September 2013

About Us
